Health & Safety Guidelines

For everyone’s safety, please follow all posted hygiene rules and listen to staff instructions during animal interactions.

Early arrival allows time for Animal Handling & Setup, safety briefing, and group organization before the exhibition begins. First Visit Special

Interactive Wildlife Experience

Bring the excitement of live animals directly to your school or event with our Mobile Zoo Exhibition. Our trained handlers create engaging, hands-on learning experiences that inspire curiosity, respect for wildlife, and unforgettable memories for children and adults alike.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Mobile Zoo Exhibition.
?

How long does a mobile zoo event last?

Most exhibitions run between 3–4 hours depending on the number of students or guests. We customize the schedule to fit your event needs.

?

Are the animals safe and well cared for?

Yes. All animals are handled by trained professionals and receive proper nutrition and veterinary care.

?

Is it safe for children?

Yes! Our trained handlers ensure safe, supervised interactions. We prioritize both guest safety and animal welfare.

?

What age groups is the mobile zoo suitable for?

Our exhibitions are perfect for preschoolers, primary schools, secondary school, and even birthday parties.

?

What space is required?

We typically require a clean, shaded outdoor area or a well-ventilated indoor space depending on the animals selected.

?

What happens in case of bad weather?

We can adjust the setup to indoor spaces where suitable or reschedule the event if necessary to ensure safety and comfort.
